# Original SFX Sources

The sound effects under `assets/sounds/ui`, `assets/sounds/enemies`, and `assets/sounds/towers` are original deterministic audio generated by this project.

Generator: `tools/generate_assets.py`
License: Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal
License URL: https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

These sounds are generated from layered oscillators, shaped noise, envelopes, and deterministic seeded noise. They do not sample third-party recordings.

Replacement map: `assets/licenses/sfx_replacement_map.json`
